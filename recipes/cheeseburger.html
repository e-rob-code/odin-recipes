Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

A delicious chedder cheeseburger with lettuce, tomato, onion. Seasoned and perfectly cooked to a beautiful medium rare. It doesn't get any better than this! Follow closely, and be the envy of the neighborhood at your next cookout.

Ingredients

  • One (1) quarter pound beef patty
  • Chedder cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Generously season the beef patty with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Cook patty on grill to a perfect medium rare
  3. Remove patty from heat, let rest for 2-3 minutes
  4. Apply thin layer of mayo on bottom bun
  5. Layer single piece of lettuce on top of mayo
  6. Layer single slice of tomato on top of lettuce
  7. Stack 2-3 thin onions on top of tomato
  8. Place slice of chedder cheese on top of burger
  9. Place burger on top of onions
  10. Apply desired amount of ketchup on top
  11. Place top on over ketchup
  12. Enjoy!
